Welcome to PhishPhry Scam Detection

  • Scammers use fake links in emails and text messages to steal personal information or money.
  • Scammers can impersonate government agencies like the IRS or Social Security Administration to ask for personal information.

  • Avoid sharing sensitive information like passwords, bank account details or Social Security Numbers.
  • Be cautious of unsolicited phone calls, mailing and door-to-door services.
